STM32CubeH7 Firmware Package V1.6.0 / 06-December-2019

Main Changes
  • Add support for stm32h7a3xx/stm32h7a3xxq/stm32h7b3xx/stm32h7b3xxq stm32h7b0xx and stm32h7b0xxq devices.

  • Add support for NUCLEO-H7A3ZI-Q, STM32H7B3I-DK and STM32H7B3I-EVAL boards.

  • Update BSP drivers in line with STM32Cube BSP drivers development guidelines (UM2298).

  • MW updates: Add Support of STM32_Network_Library.
